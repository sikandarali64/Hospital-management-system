Admission Guidelines

Follow these simple steps for a smooth and hassle-free hospital admission experience at HMS Pro.

1

Get a Doctor's Referral

Obtain a referral letter from your primary care physician or specialist. For emergency cases, this step is not required — proceed directly to our Emergency Department.

2

Registration at the Front Desk

Visit the Patient Registration counter on the ground floor (or call 111-911-911). Bring your CNIC/Passport, referral letter, and any previous medical records.

3

Initial Assessment

A nurse will perform an initial triage assessment to determine the level of care required and assign you to the appropriate department.

4

Financial Counseling

Our financial counselors will discuss estimated costs, insurance coverage, and available payment plans to ensure transparency before admission.

5

Room Assignment & Admission

Once approved, you will be assigned a room based on your medical needs — General, Semi-Private, or Private ward. Our staff will escort you and your family.

6

Discharge Planning

Discharge planning begins from day one. Our team will prepare a comprehensive discharge summary, follow-up appointments, and medication schedule.
