Welfare & Support

We believe healthcare goes beyond medicine. Discover our support programs designed to care for the whole patient.

💰

Financial Assistance

Zakat-funded programs, patient welfare funds, and installment-based payment plans are available for patients who need financial support.

🧠

Mental Health Support

Our in-house counselors and psychiatrists provide free mental health consultations and group therapy sessions for patients and their families.

🍽️

Nutritional Counseling

Registered dietitians work with patients to develop personalized meal plans that support recovery and long-term health.

🚗

Transport Assistance

We provide ambulance services and can connect patients with affordable transport options for follow-up visits.

📞

24/7 Patient Helpline

Our dedicated patient support team is available around the clock to answer medical queries, schedule appointments, and provide guidance.

🙏

Spiritual & Religious Support

A dedicated prayer room, chaplaincy services, and spiritual counselors are available for patients of all faiths during their stay.
